Searching

$ impact search <search term>

Example

$ impact search power
PowerSystems  <https://github.com/modelica/PowerSystems>
SPOT  <https://github.com/modelica-3rdparty/SPOT>
ThermoPower  <https://github.com/modelica-3rdparty/ThermoPower>

More details

$ impact search --verbose power
PowerSystems <https://github.com/modelica/PowerSystems> - The library is intended to model electrical power systems at different levels of detail both in transient and steady-state mode.
  Available versions: 0.2, 0.2.0
SPOT <https://github.com/modelica-3rdparty/SPOT> - Free library providing components to model power systems both in transient and steady-state mode.
  Available versions: None
ThermoPower <https://github.com/modelica-3rdparty/ThermoPower> - Free library to model thermal power plants (based on Modelica.Media)
  Available versions: 2.0, 2.0.0, 2.1+msl.2.2.1, 2.1+msl.2.2.2, 2.1.0+msl.2.2.1, 2.1.0+msl.2.2.2
